      TransferStateMachineException Property

      Reflects any errors which occurred during execution (null on successful completion). This property may be accessed only after the execution has completed.

      Exception

      Remarks

      In rare cases, due to inherent race conditions during parallel execution, this property might not reflect the "real" exception that occurred. This happens when:
      • Both the producer and ALL consumers fail. The producer's exception will either be its "real" exception or OperationCanceledException.
      • When the producer fails in FinalizeAsync(ITransferHasher, ITransferBuffer) (a dubious failure because it will aready have produced all data there was), "fast" consumers will complete successfully, while the "slow" ones will complete with OperationCanceledException.
